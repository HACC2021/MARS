What is MARS?

Marine Animal Reporting Service (MARS) app reports animal sightings that will give HMAR volunteers the information needed to respond to issues regarding Hawaii's marine life.

__

Who is HMAR?
Hawaii Marine Animal Response (HMAR) is the largest Hawaii-based nonprofit marine species conservation, education, field support, rescue and stranding response organization

What counts as an emergency?

  • - Animal is caught on a hook.
  • - Animal is entangled/caught in a net.
  • - Animal is visibly injured.
  • - Animal is deceased.
